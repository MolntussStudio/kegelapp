Knipapp - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Data Collection

Knipapp does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All data, including training history and settings, is stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

Knipapp does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Permissions

Knipapp requests the following permissions:

  • Notifications — to send daily training reminders
  • Vibration — for haptic feedback during exercises
  • Wake lock — to keep the screen on during exercises

Contact

If you have any questions, please contact: molntussstudio@gmail.com

Integritetspolicy (Svenska)

Knipapp samlar inte in, lagrar eller delar någon personlig data. All data, inklusive träningshistorik och inställningar, lagras endast lokalt på din enhet.

Knipapp använder inga tredjepartstjänster för analys, reklam eller spårning.
